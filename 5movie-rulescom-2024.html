  • Theatrical Distribution
    Theatrical distribution involves releasing a film in movie theaters, allowing audiences to experience it on the big screen. This traditional distribution method provides a communal viewing experience and can generate significant revenue during a film's opening weekend.
  • Streaming Distribution
    Streaming distribution has become increasingly popular in recent years, with platforms like Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, and Disney+ offering a wide range of films to subscribers.   • Home Media Distribution
    Home media distribution includes releasing a film on physical formats such as DVDs and Blu-rays, as well as digital platforms for purchase or rental. This method allows audiences to own a physical copy of a film or access it on demand, providing ongoing revenue streams for filmmakers.
  • Festival Distribution
    Festival distribution involves submitting a film to film festivals, which provide a platform for filmmakers to showcase their work to industry professionals, critics, and potential distributors. This method can generate buzz and critical acclaim, helping to secure wider distribution deals.
