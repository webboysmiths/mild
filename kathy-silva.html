Who is Kathy Silva?

Kathy Silva is an American politician and businesswoman serving as the 32nd Lieutenant Governor of Nevada since 2019. A member of the Democratic Party, she previously served in the Nevada Assembly from 2012 to 2019, representing the 23rd district.

Silva was born in Las Vegas, Nevada, and graduated from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, with a degree in business administration. She worked in the private sector before entering politics, serving as the president of the Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department Foundation and the vice president of the Nevada Resort Association.

Silva was elected to the Nevada Assembly in 2012 and served on the Assembly's Commerce and Labor Committee, the Education Committee, and the Government Affairs Committee. She was also the chair of the Assembly's Legislative Operations and Elections Committee.

In 2018, Silva was elected Lieutenant Governor of Nevada, defeating Republican challenger Michael Roberson. She is the first woman to hold the office of Lieutenant Governor in Nevada.
